R² landscape on (η, σ, log α) naive vs oracle, three solvers, single noiseless IPD

At each cell of the (η, σ, log α) grid we draw n_seeds samples from the Mixed-Dirichlet generator, forward-solve the CTMC for the noiseless IPD, then run NNLS to recover rates. The reported R² is the mean over seeds.

  • Mode — naive runs NNLS over the full DADA edge set; oracle hands the truth's active mask to the solver and runs NNLS only on those columns.
  • Solver — per-edge NNLS, pooled (per-junction) NNLS, or junction-shrinkage NNLS at the chosen λ.
  • R² is computed on observable, above-trace edges. Negative values clip to 0 in the colour map.
